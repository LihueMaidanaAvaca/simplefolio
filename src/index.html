About me

My name is Lihue. All my life I have liked to use the computer, brain games and artistic expression. Since 2020 I can capture these three tastes when developing a page and, soon, in mobile applications.

Since then I have learned HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, React, Redux and Nodejs. I also learned how to create databases with PostgresSQL and manipulate them with Sequelize.

In 2021 I got a certificate as a FullStack Developer at the SoyHenry bootcamp. Also, I was a TA (teaching assistant) for two months, to help a group of ten people to understand the topics exposed daily in the bootcamp and, at the same time, introduce them to the SCRUM methodology.

Projects

Individual Proyect (Responsive)

Dogapp is a catalog of dog breeds made with Javascript, React, Redux, CSS, Nodejs, PostgresSQL and Sequelize. It allows the user to search by name, sort items alphabetically and by breed weight, and filter by personality, by RestApi calls or saved in the database. It has the possibility of saving new breeds in the database which, when clicking on them, will show their details like the rest of the breeds. The app is fully responsive for both smartphones and tablets.
